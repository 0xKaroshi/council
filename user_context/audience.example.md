# Audience

Who follows you, who buys from you, and how they describe themselves.

## Size + composition

Total followers across handles. Rough breakdown by platform.
What % are active vs passive, what % are buyers vs lurkers.

## Persona

How a typical paying customer describes themselves. Roles, life
stage, what they're trying to do. Avoid generic personas —
specific is what makes mentor advice land.

## What pulls them in

The post / thread / piece of content that consistently does
above-baseline numbers. The thing they screenshot.

## What they actually pay for

The product / offer they convert on. Distinct from what they
engage with — engagement and conversion often diverge.

## Open audience questions

Cohorts you're trying to reach, segments you've under-served,
ideas you've parked because you couldn't tell if they'd resonate.
