# Business

What you do, who pays you, what stage you're at, what's working
and what isn't. The context the mentors don't have but you do.

## What you do

The specific work — products, services, channels. Concrete
enough that "what should I do next?" has surface to land on.

## Stage

Pre-revenue, early revenue, scaling, established — and roughly
what shape that means in your case. ARR / MRR ranges if relevant
(though not strictly required; mentors mostly need to know
"early" vs "mature").

## What's working

The thing you'd do more of if you had more hours. The leading
indicator that's actually moving.

## What's stuck

The thing that's flat or getting worse. The reason you're asking
mentors questions in the first place.

## Recent decisions

Last 30 days of meaningful changes. Helps the council see direction,
not just position.
