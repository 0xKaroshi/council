# Open strategic questions

The questions you're actively pressure-testing. Living list —
edit as questions resolve and new ones surface.

When the council reads this, it knows which uncertainties to bias
its retrieval and synthesis toward, vs. which questions are
already settled.

## Open

1. **One-line question.**
   Two or three sentences of context — what makes this question
   live, what you've ruled out, what you'd need to see to commit
   either way.

2. **Another open question.**
   Same shape. Try to phrase it as a decision the council could
   actually weigh in on, not a vague topic.

## Recently resolved (keep ~3 here for context)

- Question that you closed last week, with the one-line answer.
- Question that you closed last month, with the one-line answer.
